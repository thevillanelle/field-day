Why Play

The game is the excuse. The connection is the point.

🌍
Cross-geo always
You will never be matched with someone from your own location. Austin meets Singapore. Raleigh meets Elk Grove. The distance is the feature.
🎯
Matched to your pairing
Your game suggestion is built from the combination of both players' vibe types, drawing preferences, and session lengths. Not random. Intentional.
⏱️
Async by design
Every game in the library works without both people online at the same time. Play when it works for you. Share the moment after.
📬
The postcard model
Every interaction is a postcard. A score. A screenshot. A reaction. It travels across time zones without requiring both people to be online. Small, personal, real.
Two Modes

Stamp or The Bell.

🗺️
Stamp Mode
Intentional matching
Take the quiz → get matched by the algorithm → match drops on Monday → week-long play window → postcard submit. Curated, vibe-aware, cross-geo. The whole experience.
⚡
The Bell
Instant drop-in
A live window opens for your org. You drop in. You're instantly matched with the next available person from the opposite region. No quiz required. Pure collision.
