How It Works

Make something together — from two different places.

Step 01
🌍

Get Matched

Cross-geo pairing. Your make activity is suggested based on both your creative preferences and session lengths.

Step 02
🎨

Make Your Part

Each person contributes their half of the project. Async — you go when it works for you. No live session needed.

Step 03
📬

Share the Result

Send your contribution as a postcard. See what your match made. The combination is the artifact — two people, two places, one thing.

Activity Library

What you could make together.

Each activity produces something real — something that wouldn't exist without both of you.

Music
Collaborative Playlist
Each person adds 5 songs that represent their current mood or place. Combine into one shared playlist. The contrast tells the story.
🎵 Spotify or Apple Music⏱ 15 min
Visual
Mood Board Exchange
Both players build a small mood board (3–5 images) around a shared theme — "where I find calm," "what energy looks like." Share and compare.
🖼️ Any tool or photos⏱ 20 min
Writing
Two-Voice Story
Person A writes the opening paragraph of a story. Person B writes the next. Go back and forth until you have something. Neither of you knows where it's going.
💬 Slack or any doc⏱ Any pace
Photography
Same Prompt, Different Place
Both players photograph the same subject — "your current view," "something that represents where you live," "something old." Compare the two images side by side.
📱 Phone camera⏱ 15 min
Design
Six-Word Memoir Exchange
Each person writes a six-word memoir about their week, their year, or their life. Share both. Optionally, design a small card around each other's words.
✏️ No tools needed⏱ 10 min
Maps
Annotated Map
Each person annotates a map of their city or neighborhood — favorite spots, places that matter, things people don't know about. Share the marked-up maps.
🗺️ Google Maps or screenshot⏱ 20 min
