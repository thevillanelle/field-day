How It Works

Here's how it actually works.

Step 01
🧭

Tell us about yourself

Take a short quiz for whichever Passport feels right. A few questions about how you connect, what you're into, and what you're looking for. Takes two minutes.

Step 02
🌍

We find your match

Our algorithm pairs you with someone from a different location whose vibe complements yours. You'll never be matched with someone from your own city — the distance is the point.

Step 03
✨

Connect on your time

Play a game, answer a question, make something, or share a skill. Everything is async — no live calls, no scheduling. You go when it works for you.

Step 04
📬

Leave something behind

A score, a note, a photo, a reaction. Send your match a postcard — something small that says you showed up. That's your stamp.
