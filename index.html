How It Works

Four steps.
One new connection.

1
🧭

Take the Quiz

Six quick questions that tell us how you play, what you're into, and where you're coming from. Takes two minutes.

2
🌍

Get Matched

You're paired with someone from a different location whose vibe complements yours. Austin meets Singapore. Raleigh meets Elk Grove.

3
🎮

Play Your Game

You get a game suggestion built for your specific pairing. Play on your own time — no scheduling, no live calls required.

4
📬

Send a Postcard

Screenshot your score, a great moment, or just a message. Drop it to your match. No pressure — just a small thing that says "I was here."
