How It Works

Skip the small talk.

The question does the hard work. You just have to answer it honestly.

Step 01
🧭

Get Matched

Take the quiz once. The algorithm matches you cross-geo with someone whose vibe complements yours.

Step 02
💬

Receive Your Prompt

Both players get the same prompt — a question designed to surface something real. You each answer it independently on your own time.

Step 03
📬

Send Your Postcard

Share your answer with your match. Read theirs. Respond if you want to. That's the connection — two honest answers from two different places in the world.

Prompt Library

Questions worth answering.

Your match gets the same prompt. Your answers arrive in each other's inboxes like letters from somewhere new.

Work & Craft
"What's something you built or made that you're quietly proud of?"
Doesn't have to be at work. Could be anything.
Place & Roots
"What's one thing about where you're from that most people don't know?"
The thing you'd tell someone who actually asked.
Curiosity
"What's a rabbit hole you've fallen down recently that you didn't expect?"
The Wikipedia spiral. The late-night read. The obsession.
Perspective
"What's something you believed for a long time that you eventually changed your mind about?"
No judgment. Just honest.
Right Now
"What's something you're trying to get better at — and why now?"
Work, life, anything. What's the current project on yourself?
Texture
"Describe your ideal Tuesday. Not a vacation — just a regular day done right."
The small things reveal a lot about a person.
Connection
"What's a piece of advice you've received that you think about more than the person who gave it probably knows?"
The sentence that stuck.
Exchange
"If you could teach your match one thing you know well, what would it be?"
Sets up a Learn match naturally — but works on its own too.
